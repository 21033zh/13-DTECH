ABOUT US

WHO ARE WE?

Doll Planet is a small, NZ based business that sells handmade and preloved clothing and accessories, as well as original art prints! We specialise in vintage and eccentric womens clothing and accessories. We priorotise sustainability and quality, because we believe that fashion should be acessibile and affordable for everyone, without harming the enviorment in the process.
