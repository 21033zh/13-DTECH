Doll Planet Privacy Policy

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of personal information when you interact with our store:

  • Account details: Name, email address, and password (handled securely by Firebase Authentication).
  • Order details: Products purchased, order history, returns, and exchanges.
  • Payment information: Processed securely through Stripe Checkout (we never see or store your full card details).
  • Website usage data: IP address, browser type, device information, and cookies to improve your shopping experience.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Create and manage your account securely via Firebase Authentication.
  • Process and deliver orders.
  • Facilitate payments through Stripe Checkout.
  • Provide customer support and respond to inquiries.
  • Send order confirmations, updates, and (if you opt-in) promotional offers.
  • Improve our website, services, and product offerings.

Third-Party Services

We work with trusted third-party providers to operate our store:

  • Firebase (Google): Provides secure authentication and database services. Firebase stores your account details and order records in a protected environment.
  • Stripe: Handles all payment processing. Stripe securely processes your card information in compliance with PCI-DSS standards. We do not store full payment details on our servers.
  • Other service providers: May include shipping carriers, email providers, and analytics tools.

Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

Firebase and Stripe use industry-standard encryption and security measures to safeguard your information.

While we work to protect your data, no method of online transmission is 100% secure.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, update, or correct your personal information.
  • Request deletion of your account or data (subject to legal and transactional obligations).
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time by clicking “unsubscribe” or contacting us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with the updated effective date.
